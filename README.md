# UpGrad-dataanalysis-excel
**Tools Used**:
Microsoft excel, pivot tables, conditonal formatting
##Assignment: High profit and loss making sub-categories
*observations*:
 **High profit categories**:
 --> Telephones, Office Machines and Binders were top 3 profitable sub-categories across most regions
 --> Exception 1: Office Machines had negative profit  in West region but top in remaining regions
 --> Exception 2: Nunavut had lowest profits across all sub-categories compared to other regions
 **Loss Making Categories:**
 --> Tables and Bookcases were most loss-making sub-categories in overall
 --> Tables had biggest loss in Ontario (-42,422.71)
 --> Bookcases had biggest loss in Northwest Territories (-14,323.01)
 **Hypothesis - Tables & Chairs:**
 --> Customers tend to buy both Tables and Chairs together
 --> Here Tables act as Loss Leader product
 --> Stopping Tables may negatively impact Chairs & Chairmats sales which are profitable
 --> Recommendation: Continue selling Tables
